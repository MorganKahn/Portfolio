About Me

Hey, I'm Morgan! I am a graduate student at the University of Maryland studying human-computer interaction. I have a background in human factors psychology and am particularly interested in user research, human factors engineering, and data analytics. I have previously worked on human factors research projects involving user accessibility and education technology, and I am passionate about creating accessible and ethical human-centered solutions for information problems.
